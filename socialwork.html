Social Work Career Pathway

Explore various fields in social work, including community outreach, medical social work, and mental health support. Learn about the required skills, education, and job opportunities.

Fields of Social Work

  • Community Outreach
  • Medical Social Work
  • Mental Health Support
  • Child and Family Services
  • School Social Work

Required Skills

  • Empathy
  • Communication
  • Problem-solving
  • Organizational skills
  • Resilience

Education

Most social work careers require at least a bachelor's degree in social work (BSW). Some positions may require a master's degree (MSW) or additional certifications.

Job Opportunities

Social workers can work in various settings, including hospitals, schools, community organizations, and government agencies.
