Ayurveda Career Pathway

Explore various fields in Ayurveda, including clinical practice, research, and wellness consultancy. Learn about the required skills, education, and job opportunities.

Fields of Ayurveda

  • Clinical Practice
  • Ayurvedic Research
  • Wellness Consultancy
  • Herbal Medicine
  • Panchakarma Therapy

Required Skills

  • In-depth knowledge of Ayurvedic principles
  • Diagnostic skills
  • Herbal and pharmacological expertise
  • Patient care and empathy
  • Communication
  • Research and analytical skills

Education

Most careers in Ayurveda require a bachelor’s degree in Ayurveda (BAMS) or a related field. Advanced positions may require a master’s degree or specialized certifications.

Job Opportunities

Professionals can work in a variety of sectors, including:

  • Ayurvedic clinics
  • Hospitals
  • Wellness centers
  • Research institutions
  • Herbal product companies
