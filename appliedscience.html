Applied Science Career Pathway

Discover various fields in applied science, including biomedical engineering, environmental science, and materials science. Learn about the required skills, education, and job opportunities.

Fields of Applied Science

  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Environmental Science
  • Materials Science
  • Biotechnology
  • Forensic Science

Required Skills

  • Problem-solving
  • Analytical thinking
  • Technical knowledge
  • Teamwork
  • Communication

Education

Most applied science careers require at least a bachelor's degree in a relevant field. Some positions may require advanced degrees or certifications.

Job Opportunities

Applied scientists can work in a variety of industries, including healthcare, environmental services, and research and development.
