Required Skills

  • Analytical thinking
  • Attention to detail
  • Technical skills
  • Communication
  • Problem-solving

Education

Most forensic science careers require at least a bachelor's degree in forensic science or a related field. Some positions may require advanced degrees or certifications.

Job Opportunities

Forensic science professionals can work in law enforcement agencies, forensic laboratories, medical facilities, and research institutions.
