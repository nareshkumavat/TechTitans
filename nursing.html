Nursing Career Pathway

Explore various fields in nursing, including clinical practice, research, and education. Learn about the required skills, education, and job opportunities.

Fields of Nursing

  • Clinical Practice
    • Registered Nurse (RN)
    • Nurse Practitioner (NP)
    • Clinical Nurse Specialist (CNS)
  • Research and Development
    • Research Nurse
    • Clinical Trials Coordinator
    • Nurse Educator
  • Public Health
    • Community Health Nurse
    • Public Health Nurse
    • Health Policy Nurse
  • Specialized Nursing
    • Pediatric Nurse
    • Geriatric Nurse
    • Oncology Nurse
  • Administrative Roles
    • Nurse Manager
    • Nurse Administrator
    • Chief Nursing Officer (CNO)

Required Skills

  • Compassion
  • Attention to detail
  • Technical skills
  • Communication
  • Problem-solving

Education

Most nursing careers require at least a bachelor's degree in Nursing (BSN). Some positions may require advanced degrees or certifications.

Job Opportunities

Nursing professionals can work in hospitals, clinics, research institutions, and public health organizations.
