Physiotherapy Career Pathway

Explore various fields in physiotherapy, including clinical practice, research, and education. Learn about the required skills, education, and job opportunities.

Fields of Physiotherapy

  • Clinical Practice
    • Physical Therapist
    • Sports Physiotherapist
    • Pediatric Physiotherapist
  • Research and Development
    • Research Scientist
    • Clinical Trials Coordinator
    • Lecturer
  • Rehabilitation
    • Rehabilitation Specialist
    • Occupational Therapist
    • Speech Therapist
  • Sports and Fitness
    • Sports Therapist
    • Fitness Trainer
    • Exercise Physiologist
  • Administrative Roles
    • Clinic Manager
    • Health Services Manager
    • Rehabilitation Director

Required Skills

  • Analytical thinking
  • Technical skills
  • Problem-solving
  • Communication
  • Teamwork

Education

Most physiotherapy careers require at least a bachelor's degree in Physiotherapy (BPT). Some positions may require advanced degrees or certifications.

Job Opportunities

Physiotherapy professionals can work in hospitals, clinics, sports organizations, and rehabilitation centers.
