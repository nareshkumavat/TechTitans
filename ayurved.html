Required Skills

Education

Most Ayurved careers require at least a bachelor's degree in Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS). Some positions may require advanced degrees or certifications.

Job Opportunities

Ayurved professionals can work in clinics, wellness centers, research institutions, and educational organizations.
