Pharmacy Career Pathway

Explore various fields in pharmacy, including community pharmacy, hospital pharmacy, and research. Learn about the required skills, education, and job opportunities.

Fields of Pharmacy

  • Community Pharmacy
  • Hospital Pharmacy
  • Clinical Pharmacy
  • Research and Development
  • Regulatory Affairs

Required Skills

  • Attention to detail
  • Communication
  • Problem-solving
  • Customer service
  • Scientific knowledge

Education

Most pharmacy careers require a Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) degree. Some positions may require additional certifications or specialized training.

Job Opportunities

Pharmacists can work in various settings, including community pharmacies, hospitals, research institutions, and regulatory agencies.
