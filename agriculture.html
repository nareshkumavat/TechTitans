Agriculture Career Pathway

Explore various fields in agriculture, including agribusiness, animal science, and environmental services. Learn about the required skills, education, and job opportunities.

Required Skills

  • Analytical thinking
  • Technical skills
  • Problem-solving
  • Communication

Education

Most agriculture careers require at least a bachelor's degree in a relevant field. Some positions may require advanced degrees or certifications.

Job Opportunities

Agriculture professionals can work in a variety of settings, including farms, research institutions, and environmental organizations.
