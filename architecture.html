Architecture Career Pathway

Explore various fields in architecture, including landscape, urban planning, and restoration. Learn about the required skills, education, and job opportunities.

    • Environmental Planning
    • Recreational Design
  • Urban Planning

    • Transportation Planner

  • Restoration Architecture
    • Historic Preservation

  • Interior Architecture
    • Residential Design
    • Commercial Design
    • Sustainable Design
  • Industrial Architecture
    • Factory Design
    • Warehouse Design
    • Logistics Planning

Required Skills

  • Creative thinking
  • Technical drawing
  • Project management
  • Communication
  • Problem-solving

Education

Most architecture careers require at least a bachelor's degree in Architecture (B.Arch). Some positions may require advanced degrees or certifications.

Job Opportunities

Architects can work in design firms, construction companies, government agencies, and educational institutions.
